Domestic Peace

We are domestic peace, a year 9 Hight Resolves Project dedicated to inspiring and creating social action to stop Domestic Violence in our community.

Our projects include:

  • StoptheCycle ad.
  • Social Media Campaign.

  • Putting up posters in our community to inspire action.

  • Donation drive to help those affected by domestic violence.

  • A presentation to educate about the Cycle of Domestic Violence.

  • Fundraising project selling small items to raise money for a charity that supports victims of domestic violence. Also having people pledge to help stop domestic violence

  • Videos that help make aware the sometimes hidden issue of domestic violence

Projects that have been completed are marked with a

Domestic Peace is a project by Year 9 Chatswood High School High Resolves 2016.
